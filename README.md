# Customer Segmentation using K-Means Clustering

## Overview

This project applies **K-Means Clustering**, an unsupervised machine learning algorithm, to segment mall customers based on their **Annual Income** and **Spending Score**. The objective is to identify distinct customer groups that help businesses design personalized marketing strategies and improve customer engagement.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses often have customers with different purchasing behaviors. Instead of treating every customer the same, customer segmentation helps identify groups with similar characteristics, allowing companies to deliver targeted promotions, improve customer retention, and maximize revenue.

---

## Dataset

**Source:** Mall Customer Segmentation Dataset (Kaggle)

**Features**

- CustomerID
- Gender
- Age
- Annual Income (k$)
- Spending Score (1–100)

## Results

The K-Means algorithm successfully grouped customers into **five distinct segments** based on purchasing behavior.

These customer segments can be used for:

- Personalized Marketing
- Customer Retention
- Product Recommendations
- Business Decision Making
